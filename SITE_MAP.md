## Content Priority by Page

### Home Page
Priority: **CRITICAL**
- Must be completed first
- Sets tone for entire site
- Primary entry point for visitors

### Safety & Preparedness
Priority: **HIGH**
- Core mission of fire district
- High public interest
- Can save lives

### About Section
Priority: **HIGH**
- Transparency and accountability
- Required for governance
- Frequently referenced

### Meetings & Events
Priority: **HIGH**
- Legal requirement for public meetings
- Regular updates needed
- Community engagement

### Transparency
Priority: **HIGH**
- Legal requirement
- Public records access
- Builds trust

### Resources
Priority: **MEDIUM**
- Supporting content
- Can be built incrementally
- Links can be added over time

## User Flows

### Flow 1: New Resident
1. Lands on Home
2. Clicks "Know Your District"
3. Views District Map
4. Returns to About section
5. Reads about services
6. Downloads preparedness guide

### Flow 2: Concerned Citizen (Governance)
1. Lands on Home
2. Clicks "Transparency" or "Meetings"
3. Views upcoming meeting info
4. Downloads meeting agenda
5. Reads past meeting minutes
6. Reviews current budget

### Flow 3: Emergency Preparedness
1. Lands on Home (or directly on Safety page via search)
2. Navigates to Safety & Preparedness
3. Reads wildfire preparedness
4. Downloads go-bag checklist
5. Signs up for alerts (if available)

### Flow 4: Meeting Participant
1. Searches for "Vista Fire District meeting"
2. Lands on Meetings page
3. Views calendar
4. Finds next meeting details
5. Adds to personal calendar
6. Reads public participation rules

- ARIA landmarks for screen readers
