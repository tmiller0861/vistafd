Our Mission

The Vista Fire District is committed to protecting lives, property, and the environment through professional fire suppression, emergency medical services, fire prevention, and community education.

We strive to provide the highest level of service to our community through continuous training, modern equipment, and a dedication to excellence in emergency response.

Our Services

Fire Suppression

24/7 emergency response to structural fires, wildland fires, and other fire-related emergencies.

Emergency Medical Services

Advanced life support and basic life support emergency medical response.

Fire Prevention

Fire safety inspections, code enforcement, and fire investigation services.

Public Education

Fire safety education, school programs, and community outreach initiatives.
